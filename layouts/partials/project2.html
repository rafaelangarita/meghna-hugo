Collaborative Editing for Large Scale Online Citizen Participation

A key challenge of Civic Tech lies in providing digital tools enabling individuals to engage in participatory processes through the collaborative edition of proposals on topics that matter to society. The focus of this research is to overcome such a challenge by leveraging and further adapting techniques that have proven successful in open source software development.
